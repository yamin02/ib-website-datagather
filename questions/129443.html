Question

Let \(\sin \theta  = \frac{{\sqrt 5 }}{3}\), where \(\theta \) is acute.

Find \(\cos \theta \).

[3]
a.

Find \(\cos 2\theta \).

[2]
b.

Markscheme

evidence of valid approach     (M1)

eg\(\,\,\,\,\,\)right triangle, \({\cos ^2}\theta  = 1 - {\sin ^2}\theta \)

correct working     (A1)

eg\(\,\,\,\,\,\)missing side is 2, \(\sqrt {1 - {{\left( {\frac{{\sqrt 5 }}{3}} \right)}^2}} \)
