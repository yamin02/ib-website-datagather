Question

The expression \(6\sin x\cos x\) can be expressed in the form \(a\sin bx\) .

Find the value of a and of b .

[3]
a.

Hence or otherwise, solve the equation \(6\sin x\cos x = \frac{3}{2}\) , for \(\frac{\pi }{4} \le x \le \frac{\pi }{2}\) .

[4]
b.

\(a = 3\) , \(b = 2\)     A1A1     N3
