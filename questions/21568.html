Question

The following diagram shows the graph of \(y = f(x)\), for \( - 4 \le x \le 5\).


Write down the value of \(f( - 3)\).

[1]
a(i).

Write down the value of  \({f^{ - 1}}(1)\).

[1]
a(ii).

Find the domain of \({f^{ - 1}}\).

[2]
b.

On the grid above, sketch the graph of \({f^{ - 1}}\).

[3]
c.
