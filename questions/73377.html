Question

Let \(f(x) = 2\ln (x - 3)\), for \(x > 3\). The following diagram shows part of the graph of \(f\).

Find the equation of the vertical asymptote to the graph of \(f\).

[2]
a.

Find the \(x\)-intercept of the graph of \(f\).

[2]
b.
