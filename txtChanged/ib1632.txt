<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">correct substitution into sum of a geometric sequence&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <em><strong>A1</strong> </em></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;"><em>eg &nbsp;</em> \(62.755 = {u_1}\left( {\frac{{1 - {r^3}}}{{1 - r}}} \right)\) , \({u_1} + {u_1}r + {u_1}{r^2} = 62.755\)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">correct substitution into sum to infinity&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <em><strong>A1</strong> </em></span></p>

<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">attempt to eliminate one variable&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<em><strong> (M1)</strong> </em></span></p>
