<p>valid approach to find \({\rm{A\hat EC}}\) (may be seen in (a)) &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(M1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\,\,\,\,\,\)\({\rm{A\hat EC}} = 180 - 55 - {\rm{A\hat CE}},{\text{ }}134 = {\text{E}} + 55\)</p>
<p>correct working to find \({\rm{A\hat EC}}\) (may be seen in (a)) &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(A1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\,\,\,\,\,\)\(180 - 55 - 46,{\text{ }}134 - 55\), \({\rm{A\hat EC}} = 79^\circ \)</p>
<p>evidence of choosing sine rule (seen anywhere) &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(M1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\,\,\,\,\,\)\(\frac{a}{{\sin A}} = \frac{b}{{\sin B}}\)</p>
<p>correct substitution into sine rule &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(A1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\,\,\,\,\,\)\(\frac{{{\text{CE}}}}{{\sin 55^\circ }} = \frac{{175}}{{\sin {\rm{A\hat EC}}}}\)</p>
<p>146.034</p>
<p>\({\text{CE}} = 146{\text{ (km)}}\) &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>A1</em></strong> &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>N2</em></strong></p>

<p>evidence of choosing cosine rule &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(M1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\,\,\,\,\,\)\({\text{D}}{{\text{E}}^2} = {\text{D}}{{\text{C}}^2} + {\text{C}}{{\text{E}}^2} - 2 \times {\text{DC}} \times {\text{CE}} \times \cos \theta \)</p>
<p>correct substitution into right-hand side &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(A1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\,\,\,\,\,\)\({60^2} + {146.034^2} - 2 \times 60 \times 146.034\cos 134\)</p>

<p>valid approach for expressing time &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(M1)</em></strong></p>

<p>correct working equating time &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(A1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\,\,\,\,\,\)\(\frac{{146.034\sin 46^\circ }}{r} = \frac{{192.612}}{{50}},{\text{ }}\frac{s}{{105.048}} = \frac{{50}}{{192.612}}\)</p>
