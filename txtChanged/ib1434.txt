<p>(i) &nbsp; &nbsp; Write down the area of the sector when \(\theta&nbsp; = \alpha \).</p>
<p>(ii) &nbsp; &nbsp; Hence find \(\alpha \).</p>

<div class='question' style='padding-left: 20px; padding-right: 20px;'>
<p>When \(\theta&nbsp; = \beta \), the area of \(R\) is more than twice the area of the sector.</p>
<p>Find all possible values of \(\beta \).</p>

<div class='question' style='padding-left: 20px;'>
<p>area of \({\text{ABCD}} = {\text{A}}{{\text{B}}^2}\) (seen anywhere) &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(A1)</em></strong></p>
<p>choose cosine rule to find a side of the square &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(M1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\;\;\;{a^2} = {b^2} + {c^2} - 2bc\cos \theta \)</p>
<p>correct substitution (for triangle \(AOB\)) &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>A1</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\;\;\;{r^2} + {r^2} - 2 \times r \times r\cos \theta ,{\text{ O}}{{\text{A}}^2} + {\text{O}}{{\text{B}}^2} - 2 \times {\text{OA}} \times {\text{OB}}\cos \theta \)</p>
<p>correct working for \({\text{A}}{{\text{B}}^2}\) &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>A1</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\;\;\;2{r^{\text{2}}} - 2{r^2}\cos \theta \)</p>
<p>\({\text{area}} = 2{r^2}(1 - \cos \theta )\) &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>AG &nbsp; &nbsp; N0</em></strong></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Note: &nbsp; &nbsp; </strong>Award no marks if the only working is \(2{r^2} - 2{r^2}\cos \theta \).</p>
