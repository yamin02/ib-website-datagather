<p class="p1">The third term in the expansion of \({(x + k)^8}\) is \(63{x^6}\). Find the possible values of \(k\).</p>
</div>
$$$$
<div class='question'>
<p>valid approach to find the required term &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(M1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\;\;\;\left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} 8 \\ r \end{array}} \right){x^{8 - r}}{k^r}\)<em>, </em>Pascal&rsquo;s triangle to \({{\text{8}}^{{\text{th}}}}\) row, \({x^8} + 8{x^7}k + 28{x^6}{k^2} +&nbsp; \ldots \)</p>
<p>identifying correct term (may be indicated in expansion) &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(A1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\;\;\;\left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} 8 \\ 2 \end{array}} \right){x^6}{k^2},{\text{ }}\left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} 8 \\ 6 \end{array}} \right){x^6}{k^2},{\text{ }}r = 2\)</p>
<p>setting up equation in \(k\) with <strong>their </strong>coefficient/term &nbsp; &nbsp; <strong><em>(M1)</em></strong></p>
<p><em>eg</em>\(\;\;\;28{k^2}{x^6} = 63{x^6},{\text{ }}\left( {\begin{array}{*{20}{c}} 8 \\ 6 \end{array}} \right){k^2} = 63\)</p>
