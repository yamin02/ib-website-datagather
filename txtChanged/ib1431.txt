
<div class='question'>
<p class="p1">Ramiro walks to work each morning. During the first minute he walks&nbsp;\(80\) metres. In each subsequent minute he walks&nbsp;\(90\% \) of the distance walked during the previous minute.</p>
<p class="p1">The distance between his house and work is&nbsp;\(660\) metres. Ramiro leaves his house at 08:00 and has to be at work by 08:15.</p>
<p class="p1">Explain why he will not be at work on time.</p>
