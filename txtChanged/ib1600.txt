<p align="LEFT"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">A random variable \(X\) is normally distributed with \(\mu&nbsp; = 150\) and \(\sigma&nbsp; = 10\) .</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">Find the interquartile range of \(X\) .</span></p>

$$$$
<div class='question'>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">recognizing one quartile probability (may be seen in a sketch)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong><em>(M1)</em> </strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;"><em>eg &nbsp;</em> \({\rm{P}}(X &lt; {Q_3}) = 0.75\) , \(0.25\)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">finding standardized value for either quartile&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong><em>(A1)</em> </strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;"><em>eg &nbsp; </em>\(z = 0.67448 \ldots \) , \(z = - 0.67448 \ldots \)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">attempt to set up equation (must be with \(z\)-values)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong><em>(M1)</em> </strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;"><em>eg &nbsp;</em> \(0.67 = \frac{{{Q_3} - 150}}{{10}}\) , \( - 0.67448 = \frac{{x - 150}}{{10}}\)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">one correct quartile </span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;"><em>eg &nbsp;</em> \({Q_3} = 156.74 \ldots \) , \({Q_1} = 143.25 \ldots \)</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">correct working&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <strong><em>(A1)</em> </strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;"><em>eg&nbsp;&nbsp;</em> other correct quartile, \({Q_3} - \mu&nbsp; = 6.744 \ldots \)</span></p>
